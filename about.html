About Voyager Park

Voyager Park was established in 1987 by the Thompson family, who had a vision of creating a unique destination that would bring together adventure, nature, and family fun. What started as a small collection of hiking trails and picnic areas has grown into a world-class attraction spanning over 200 acres of beautiful landscape. The park was named "Voyager" to honor the spirit of exploration and discovery that the founders wanted every visitor to experience. Over the decades, Voyager Park has continuously expanded its offerings, adding thrilling rides, educational exhibits, and conservation programs while maintaining its commitment to preserving the natural beauty of the area. Today, the park welcomes over 500,000 visitors annually and remains a family-owned business dedicated to creating magical memories for guests from around the world.
